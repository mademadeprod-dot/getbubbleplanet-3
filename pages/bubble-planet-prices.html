Tips

How to get the best price

📅 Book weekday slots

Weekday sessions (Monday–Thursday) are typically priced lower than weekend slots and have more availability. If your schedule allows, a Tuesday or Wednesday visit is usually the best value.

🎟 Check for discount codes

Promotional discount codes are released periodically. Check our discount code page before you book — even a 10–15% off code adds up for a family visit.

👨‍👩‍👧 Kids under 2 are free

In most cities, children under 2 years of age enter for free when accompanied by a paying adult. Great news for families with toddlers — you don't need to buy them a separate ticket.

⏰ Book early for peak dates

School holidays, bank holidays and weekends sell out fastest. Book at least 1–2 weeks in advance for busy periods to guarantee your preferred time slot.
